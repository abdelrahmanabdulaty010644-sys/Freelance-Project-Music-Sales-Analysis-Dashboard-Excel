# 🎧 Music Genre vs. Beverage Revenue: Analytics-Driven Club Performance Optimization

## 🎯 Project Overview
Does the type of music played in a venue directly impact its business profitability? This data analysis project answers this precise question by evaluating business performance metrics for a commercial venue. 

The primary objective was to transform event logs into actionable commercial insights by identifying patterns between **DJs, music genres (EDM, Hip-Hop, Reggaeton), hourly attendance, and beverage revenue**. The analysis equips management with data-backed strategies to optimize entertainment scheduling and maximize operational profits.

## 🛠️ Data Methodology & Analytical Approaches

### 1. 🧹 Data Cleansing & Preparation
- Structured raw historical log files, handling hourly timestamps, adjusting missing values, and formatting structural inconsistencies within Microsoft Excel.

### 2. 📊 Statistical Correlation Analysis
- Conducted **Correlation Analysis** to measure the statistical strength and direction of the linear relationships between diverse variables (e.g., specific music genres vs. total beverage revenue generated).

### 3. 📉 Advanced Data Visualization
- Designed an interactive Excel Dashboard featuring a **Dual-Axis Chart** to visually compare temporal beverage sales against the active playback hours of different music genres. This chart synchronizes financial and entertainment metrics on a single timeline.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights & Findings

- **👑 EDM Dominance:** **EDM (Electronic Dance Music)** emerged as the highest-grossing music genre, triggering the sharpest spike in beverage spending efficiency.
- **👥 Attendance Catalyst:** **Hip-Hop** proved to be the most powerful driver for absolute volume attraction, consistently generating the highest crowd attendance.
- **🏆 Premium Performer:** **DJ Luna Eclipse** yielded the highest total revenue among all performers, strategically leveraging EDM-heavy sets to optimize sales during peak hours.
- **📈 Positive Correlation Confirmation:** The analysis proved a **strong positive correlation** between absolute crowd volume and beverage sales. Strategic music selection acts as a direct business lever to drive higher per-capita spending.
